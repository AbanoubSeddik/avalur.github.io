Take a look at our two main tracks for the festival. Track 1, 'Solo Mastery,' is a Kaggle-style individual competition where you'll be tasked with processing source code to extract key information. Details are announced on Discord, and there's no limit on the number of participants. So if you're up for the challenge, give it a try!
Moving on to Track 2, 'The Power of the Team,' we invite you to form teams of up to six people. This is your chance to assemble your dream team and create an AI companion that will transform the student learning experience.

Track 2: The Power of the Team

The task is to create a universal AI assistant that helps students solve educational programming tasks with tests (without providing the answer).

We are not setting a strict list of functionalities that AI assistant has to perform. Teams are free to experiment and seek creative solutions within the general context of creating an educational AI assistant.

Maximum six team members.

(As the text appears)
In Track 2, we challenge you to push the boundaries of collaborative innovation. Your task is to create a universal AI assistant that helps students not just solve, but truly understand educational programming tasks. This AI won't just give away the answers but will guide students through the problem-solving process with intelligent hints.
Remember, we're not prescribing a rigid set of functionalities. Your teams are encouraged to think outside the box, to be as innovative as possible within the educational context. It's all about enhancing learning while fostering creativity.
  1. Available data/tools for participants:
    • The education course (with problems, the correct solutions and tests)
    • Students' solutions
    • Access to Grazie API
  2. Assistant's working principle:
    • The student writes and runs code (any programming language) for the task
    • An error occurs (either during code execution or due to an incorrect solution)
    • AI assistant gives a hint / the user can click on the AI assistant to get a hint
(As bullet points appear)
To support your development, we're providing an array of resources. You'll have access to our education course complete with problems, correct solutions, and tests, students' solutions for insights, and the powerful Grazie API for language tools.
Your AI assistant should operate on a simple yet effective principle. When a student encounters an error in their code, whether it's during execution or an incorrect solution, your AI will step in to offer hints and nudge them in the right direction. It's about empowering students to learn from their mistakes and improve their coding skills.

Winners will be determined by a jury that evaluates the AI assistants on test data, focusing on functionality. It might be AI that can pass the 'Duck' test, explain course terms, and check code style. The format of your AI assistant can vary from a console application to a web, desktop, or mobile application. The choice is yours, as long as it effectively meets the project goals.

Don't forget to use the resources we've provided. Hyperskill and the JetBrains Academy Plugin are excellent tools to enhance your learning and development process.
When it comes to judging, we'll be looking at user experience, functionality, technical robustness, educational impact, and presentation. Each category has a set number of points, so plan your project with these criteria in mind to maximize your score.

Project Submission Process

By the designated deadline (March 3, 6:00 PM), your team is required to submit your project's progress by uploading it to a publicly accessible repository, as well as recording a video presentation. Aim to vividly and completely describe and demonstrate all of your ideas in the video, including both those you have managed to implement and those you were unable to complete. Including a README file in your project repository to guide users through the project setup process is highly recommended. The video presentation should be no longer than 5 minutes.

The links to both the repository and the video presentation will be collected through a specific form.
